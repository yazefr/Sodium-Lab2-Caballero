Roblox

It’s a fun game platform where you can play millions of different... experiences and games... specifically created for the platform. A vast majority of those experiences are games, but you can also do things like attend concerts or visit Stranger Things’ Starcourt Mall re-created in Roblox. built inside Roblox itself.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:

  • Because it's fun
  • You can make friends
  • You can design your avatar
  • You can make your imagination bigger
  • You can interact to other people
